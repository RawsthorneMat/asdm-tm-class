'''Horace Murphy''' (June 3, 1880 &ndash; January 20, 1975) was an American film actor.<ref name="NY Times">{{cite web |url=http://www.nytimes.com/movies/person/51450/Horace-Murphy |title=Horace Murphy |accessdate=December 7, 2014 |work=New York Times}}</ref> He appeared in more than 120 films between 1931 and 1953.
