'''Nathan Lee Chasing His Horse''' (born April 28, 1976) is a [[Native Americans in the United States|Native American]] actor. He is a member of the [[Lakota people|Rosebud Lakota Sioux Nation]], the son of Chief Joseph Chasing Horse and Winifred Chasing Horse.

Chasing His Horse was cast to play the part of ''Smiles A Lot'' ([[Lakota language|Lakota]]: ''Iȟá s’a'') in [[Kevin Costner|Kevin Costner's]] movie ''[[Dances with Wolves]]''. He starred in three [[Turner Network Television|TNT]] telefilms with [[First Nations]] actor [[Eric Schweig]]: ''The Broken Chain'', ''[[Into the West (TV miniseries)|Into the West]]'' and ''[[Bury My Heart at Wounded Knee (film)|Bury My Heart at Wounded Knee]]''.
