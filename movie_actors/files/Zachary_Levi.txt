'''Zachary Levi Pugh''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|z|æ|k|ər|i|_|ˈ|l|iː|v|aɪ|_|ˈ|p|juː}}; born September 29, 1980),<ref name=tvg>{{cite web | url = http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/zachary-levi/bio/191119 | title= Zachary Levi | publisher= [[TV Guide|TVGuide.com]] | accessdate= 2011-08-03}}</ref> known professionally as '''Zachary Levi''', is an American actor, director, and singer. He is best known for his roles as Kip Steadman in sitcom ''[[Less Than Perfect]]'' and as [[Chuck Bartowski]] in the series ''[[Chuck (TV series)|Chuck]]''. He appeared in the films ''[[Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Squeakquel]]'', ''[[Tangled]]'', and ''[[Thor: The Dark World]]''.

== Early life ==
Levi was born in [[Lake Charles, Louisiana]], the son of Susan and Darrell Pugh.<ref name=tvg /> He is of [[Welsh people|Welsh]] ancestry.<ref name=nyt1>{{cite news |title=Super Nerd Is Out to Save the World |work=[[The New York Times]]|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2007/09/30/arts/television/30wyat.html?pagewanted=all |accessdate=August 21, 2010 |first=Edward|last=Wyatt|date=September 30, 2007}}</ref> When Levi was a child, his family moved across several states before finally heading back to their home in [[Ventura, California]], where he attended [[Buena High School (California)|Buena High School]] for four years.<ref>{{cite episode|title=Anytime with Bob Kushell feat. Zach Levi |series=[[Anytime with Bob Kushell]] |airdate=February 5, 2009 |season=1|number=8}}</ref> He began acting on stage at the age of six, performing lead roles in regional productions such as ''[[Grease (musical)|Grease]]'', ''The Outsiders'', ''[[Oklahoma!]]'', ''[[Oliver!]]'', ''[[Adaptations of The Wizard of Oz|The Wizard of Oz]]'', and ''[[Big River (musical)|Big River]]'' at the Ojai Art Center. He chose his middle name as his [[stage name]] because his agent told him that "a guy named Zach Pugh would not be going far in Hollywood".<ref name=nyt1/>

== Career ==

=== Acting ===
[[File:ZacharyLeviCCJuly09.jpg|thumb|upright|Levi at the 2009 San Diego Comic-Con]]
Levi first appeared in the [[FX Networks|FX]] television movie ''Big Shot: Confessions of a Campus Bookie.'' He played Kipp Steadman on the [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] sitcom ''[[Less Than Perfect]]''. He also portrayed a potential boyfriend of [[Charisma Carpenter]]'s character, Jane, in the ABC Family television movie'' [[See Jane Date]].'' Levi was slated to be a lead in an ABC pilot called ''Three'' for the 2004/2005 television season, but the show was not picked up.<ref>{{cite news |last=Lisotta |first=Christopher|date=May 16, 2005|title=SPECIAL REPORT: Upfront Navigator |work=Television Week |volume=24 |issue=20 |pages=32–37}}</ref>

[[File:Zachary Levi 2011.jpg|thumb|left|upright|Levi at the [[83rd Academy Awards]] in February 2011]]

He landed the [[Chuck Bartowski|starring role]] in ''[[Chuck (TV series)|Chuck]]'' in 2007. Levi and his ''Chuck'' co-star, [[Yvonne Strahovski]], were both nominated for Best Action Actor and Actress Choice TV Series for the [[Teen Choice Awards]] 2010 where they both won and presented.<ref>{{cite news |last=Stransky |first=Tanner |title=2010 Teen Choice Awards winners announced|url=http://news-briefs.ew.com/2010/08/09/teen-choice-awards-winners-201 |accessdate=March 8, 2011 |newspaper=EW |date=August 9, 2010}}</ref> In the summer of 2008, Levi was named one of ''[[Entertainment Weekly|Entertainment Weekly']]<nowiki/>s ''Top Thirty People Under Thirty.<ref>{{cite news |first=Adam|last=Markovitz|author2=Tanner Stransky  |title=30 Under 30: The Actors |work=[[Entertainment Weekly]]|url=http://www.ew.com/ew/gallery/0,,20166944_20166999_20180615_21,00.html |date=March 3, 2008 |accessdate=March 10, 2009}}</ref> He later starred in the film ''[[Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Squeakquel]]'' in the lead role as Dave's cousin Toby Seville.
<ref>Corliss, Richard (January 11, 2010). "Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Squeakquel". ''Time''. '''175''' (1):51.</ref> In 2010 he is presented [[Kids' Choice Awards]], were both a winner for ''Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Squeakquel'' in Best Favorite Movie. He starred in the 2010 Disney-animated feature film ''[[Tangled]]'', which is based on the popular fairy tale [[Rapunzel]]. He voices Flynn Rider, a bandit who finds refuge in Rapunzel's tower. He provided narration in the 2011 film ''Under the Boardwalk: The Monopoly Story'', a documentary about the game of [[Monopoly (game)|Monopoly]].<ref>{{cite news |last=Wright |first=Anders |title=Rolling the dice|url=http://www.sdcitybeat.com/sandiego/article-8798-rolling-the-dice.html |accessdate=March 25, 2011 |newspaper=San Diego City Beat|date=March 2, 2011}}</ref> Levi hosted the 2011 [[Spike Video Game Awards]].<ref name="MTVVGAZacharyLeviInterview">{{cite web|first=Charles | last=Webb| url =http://multiplayerblog.mtv.com/2011/12/05/spike-vgas-interview-meet-your-host-zachary-levi/ | title = Spike VGAs Interview: Meet Your Host, Zachary Levi | publisher = MTV| date = 2011-12-05 | accessdate = 2011-12-07 | deadurl=no}}</ref>

[[BuddyTV]] ranked him #97 on its list of "TV's Sexiest Men of 2011".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.buddytv.com/slideshows/white-collar/tvs-100-sexiest-men-of-2011-29104.aspx|title=TV's 100 Sexiest Men of 2011|publisher=[[BuddyTV]]|accessdate=January 13, 2012}}</ref> Levi landed the male lead in the 2012 TV pilot ''Let It Go'', which would have aired on Fox, but was not picked up.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvline.com/2012/02/13/zachary-levi-let-it-go/|title=Pilot Scoop: Zach Levi to Headline Fox's Let It Go|publisher=TvLine|accessdate=February 13, 2012}}</ref>
Levi hosted the web documentaries ''Tomb Raider The Final Hours'', going behind the scenes of the new ''[[Tomb Raider (2013 video game)|Tomb Raider game]]''. He was originally to portray Fandral in ''[[Thor (film)|Thor]]'', but had to drop out due to scheduling conflicts with ''Chuck''.<ref>{{cite news|url= http://www.huffingtonpost.com/mobileweb/2012/06/09/thor-2-zachary-levi-fandral-josh-dallas_n_1583137.html|title='Thor 2': Zachary Levi Eyed For Fandral, Josh Dallas Out Of Film|work=[[huffingtonpost]]|date=June 6, 2012|accessdate=October 3, 2012}}</ref> He did play the role in the sequel, ''[[Thor: The Dark World]]'' (2013), after original actor [[Josh Dallas]] had scheduling conflicts of his own. Levi made his Broadway debut in a production of the musical comedy ''[[First Date the Musical|First Date]]'' in August 2013.<ref>{{cite news|last=Carlson|first=Adam|title=Zachary Levi made his Broadway debut in 'First Date'|url=http://popwatch.ew.com/2013/05/20/zachary-levi-broadway-first-date/|accessdate=August 19, 2013|newspaper=[[Entertainment Weekly]]|date=May 20, 2013}}</ref> 

In January 2015, it was announced that Levi will be a lead member of the cast of NBC's 13-episode miniseries ''[[Heroes Reborn (TV miniseries)|Heroes Reborn]]''; the role has yet to be named.<ref>{{cite news|last=Ausiello|first=Michael|title=Zachary Levi to Lead ''Heroes Reborn'' Miniseries on NBC|url=http://tvline.com/2015/01/16/zachary-levi-heroes-reborn-cast-nbc/|accessdate=January 16, 2015|website=[[TVLine]]|date=January 16, 2015}}</ref> He will also be guest-starring as Abraham Lincoln in the Hulu original series ''[[Deadbeat_(TV_series)|Deadbeat]]''.<ref>{{cite news|last=Ausiello|first=Michael|title=''Chuck'' Vet Zachary Levi to Play Abraham Lincoln in ''Deadbeat'' Season 2|url=http://tvline.com/2015/01/07/zachary-levi-deadbeat-season-2-abe-lincoln-hulu/|accessdate=January 16, 2015|website=[[TVLine]]|date=January 7, 2015}}</ref>

=== Music ===
[[File:Zachary Levi about working on Broadway at NerdHQ 2014.webm|thumb|Levi about his time on Broadway|thumbtime=2:00]]
In April 2010, [[Katharine McPhee]] released a preview of a music video to cinemas for her single "[[Terrified (Katharine McPhee song)|Terrified]]". Levi duets with her on the song (which was re-recorded; the original album version features [[Jason Reeves (songwriter)|Jason Reeves]]) and appears in the music video singing with McPhee. On May 3, 2010, the song was previewed on ''[[Entertainment Weekly|Entertainment Weekly']]''s website and was made available for purchase on [[iTunes]].<ref>"[http://www.katharinemcphee.com/news_d.aspx?nid=2030 Terrified featuring Zachary Levi on Entertainment Weekly]". Retrieved 2011-08-03.</ref> The full video premiered on May 7, 2010 on the music video website [[Vevo]].<ref>[http://www.katharinemcphee.com/news_d.aspx?nid=2037 "Terrified premieres on Vevo today!"]. Retrieved 2011-08-03.</ref> Levi financed [[Kendall Payne]]'s album ''Grown'' after she was dropped by her label, [[Capitol Records]].<ref>{{cite news |last=Carlozo |first=Louis R. |date=April 4, 2006|title=Grown |work=Christian Century |volume=123 |issue=7 |page=55}}</ref>

Levi is featured on the soundtrack of the movie ''[[Tangled]]'', singing a duet with co-star [[Mandy Moore]] called "I See the Light", as well as being featured in the song "I've Got a Dream". In honor of the Oscar nomination for Best Original Song, Levi and Moore performed "I See the Light" at the [[83rd Academy Awards]].<ref name="Skerry ">{{cite news |last=Skerry |first=Kath |title=Zachary Levi Sings at the Oscars (Video)|url=http://www.givememyremote.com/remote/2011/02/27/zachary-levi-sings-at-the-oscars |accessdate=March 8, 2011 |newspaper=[[Give Me My Remote]] |date=February 27, 2011}}</ref> He has also been a guest member of [[Band from TV]], a band made up of actors from various American television shows.

== Personal life ==
Levi is a sports car and motorcycle enthusiast and owns a 2009 [[Nissan GT-R]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.adamcarolla.com/CarCastBlog/2010/07/16/zach-levi |title=Zach Levi |last=Carolla |first=Adam |date=July 16, 2010|work=[[CarCast]] |accessdate=September 12, 2010}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |last=Nygaard |first=Sandra |date=October 2007 |title=With a name like Levi, he's gotta know jeans |work=Men's Health |volume=22 |issue=8 |page=134}}</ref> He is an avid gamer, having first played ''[[Super Mario Bros.]]'', and has come to appreciate video games for their ability to provide interactive stories.<ref name="MTVVGAZacharyLeviInterview" />

Levi is a [[Christian]] and has been since childhood. In a 2002 interview with ''[[Relevant Magazine]]'', he said: "My job on my set, I believe, is to first just love people and gain that trust with people where they know that I really do love them and care about their well-being, so that when they are running into problems, they will hopefully, at some point, come to me and ask me, 'What is your peace all about? What is your comfort all about? Where do you get your love? Where do you get your talents?' And I can turn to them and say without blinking, '[[Jesus Christ]].{{'"}}<ref>{{cite news |title=Q&A With Zachary Levi |work=Relevant Magazine|url=http://www.relevantmagazine.com/culture/film/features/2830-qaa-with-zachary-levi |accessdate=October 13, 2009}}</ref>

In September 2010, Levi started his own company, [[The Nerd Machine]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Zac Launches The Nerd Machine|work=ChuckTV.net|url=http://chucktv.net/2010/09/24/zac-launches-the-nerd-machine |accessdate=September 24, 2010}}</ref> He was selected as a participant in the Celebrity All Star Game at [[2011 NBA All-Star Game|NBA All Star Weekend 2011]]. He played for the team coached by [[Magic Johnson]].<ref>{{cite news|first=Rich|last=Sands|title=Justin Bieber, Ty Burrell and Zachary Levi Hoop It Up for NBA All-Star Celebrity Game|url=http://www.tvguide.com/News/Justin-Bieber-Ty-1028767.aspx|accessdate=October 3, 2012|newspaper=[[TV Guide]]|date=February 1, 2011}}</ref>

On June 16, 2014, [[Missy Peregrym]] announced via Twitter that she and Levi had gotten married in [[Maui, Hawaii]] along with posting a photo of them in matching Mr. and Mrs. hoodies.<ref name=marriage>{{cite web|last=Marquina |first=Sierra |url=http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/zachary-levi-secretly-marries-girlfriend-missy-peregrym-in-maui-pic-2014166 |title=Zachary Levi Secretly Marries Girlfriend Missy Peregrym In Maui: Pic – Us Weekly |publisher=Usmagazine.com |accessdate=2014-06-17}}</ref> The marriage was short-lived and the pair filed for divorce in April of 2015, with the separation date listed in court documents as December 3, 2014.<ref name=divorce>{{cite web|url=http://www.tmz.com/2015/04/18/zachary-levi-missy-peregrym-divorce-secretly-married/ |title=Zachary Levi & Missy Peregrym Secret Wedding Ends In Even More Secret Divorce |publisher=TMZ.com |date= |accessdate=2015-04-18}}</ref>
