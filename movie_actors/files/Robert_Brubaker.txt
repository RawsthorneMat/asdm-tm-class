'''Robert Brubaker''' (October 9, 1916 â€“ April 15, 2010) was an [[United States|American]] [[character actor]] best known for his roles in [[television]] and movie [[westerns]], including as ''[[Gunsmoke]]'' and ''[[40 Guns to Apache Pass]]''. Brubaker was the only actor to have two recurring roles on the television series, ''Gunsmoke'', portraying both a [[bartender]] named Floyd and a [[stagecoach]] driver named Jim Buck.  Some of Brubaker's other credits included the Rock Hudson film, ''[[Seconds (film)|Seconds]]'', and television crime drama ''[[The Walter Winchell File]]'' and the television police drama ''[[The Asphalt Jungle (TV series)|The Asphalt Jungle]]''.

Robert Brubaker was born in [[Robinson, Illinois]], on October 9, 1916.  He died on April 15, 2010, at the age of 93.
