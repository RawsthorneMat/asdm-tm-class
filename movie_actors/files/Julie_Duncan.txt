'''Julie Duncan''' (born 17 January 1919 in [[Cornish, New Hampshire]]; died 20 June 1986<ref>http://www.threestooges.net/cast.php?id=338</ref>) was a motion picture actress specialising in [[short subject]]s and [[Western (genre)|Western]]s.  She was a champion [[steeplechase]] rider.<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0242004/bio</ref>
