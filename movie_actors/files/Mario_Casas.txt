'''Mario Casas Sierra'''(born in [[A Coruña]], [[Province of A Coruña|A Coruña]], Galicia, Spain on 12 June 1986) is a Spanish actor. He is probably best known for his performances in television series ''[[Los hombres de Paco|Paco's Men]]'' (Spanish: ''Los hombres de Paco'') and ''[[El Barco (TV series)|The Boat]]'' (''El barco''), as well as in films ''[[Summer Rain (2006 film)|Summer Rain]]'' (''El camino de los ingleses''), ''[[Three Steps Above Heaven]]'' (''Tres metros sobre el cielo'') and ''I Want You'' (''Tengo ganas de ti'').

== Acting career ==
Mario Casas began his acting career with episode appearances in television series ''[[:es:Obsesión (telenovela de 2005)|Obsesión]]'' (English: ''Obsession''), ''[[Motivos personales]]'' (''Personal Motives'') and ''[[:es:Mujeres (serie de televisión)|Mujeres]]'' (''Women''). He came to domestic media attention with his film debut in the [[mario es guapo jsjsjsjs in film|2006 film]] ''[[Summer Rain (2006 film)|El camino de los ingleses]]'' (''Summer Rain''), directed by [[Antonio Banderas]] who gave him the opportunity to participate. 

That same year he joined the cast of [[LaSexta]] series ''[[:es:SMS (serie de televisión)|SMS]]''. Casas then reached international audience as Aitor Carrasco in the [[Antena 3 (Spain)|Antena 3]] series ''[[Los hombres de Paco]]'' (''Paco's Men''), which he starred from 2007 to 2010. Later in 2009 Casas appeared in two hit films, ''[[Fuga de cerebros]]'' (''Brain Drain'') opposite [[Amaia Salamanca]], and ''[[:es:Mentiras y gordas|Mentiras y gordas]]'' (''Sex, Lies and Parties'') with he's bestfriend [[Yon González]].<ref name="pec"/> Both managed to lead the box office over the weekend of its release.

In 2009, Casas appeared in two hit films, ''[[Fuga de cerebros]]'' (''Brain Drain'') opposite [[Amaia Salamanca]], and ''[[:es:Mentiras y gordas|Mentiras y gordas]]'' (''Sex, Lies and Parties'') with [[Yon González]], [[Ana Maria Polvorosa]], [[Ana de Armas]] and [[Hugo Silva]]. In 2010, Casas starred ''[[Three Meters Above The Sky|Tres metros sobre el cielo]]'' (''Three Steps Above Heaven'') opposite [[María Valverde]]. It was eventually the highest-grossing Spanish film of the year. A sequel [[Tengo ganas de ti]]'' (''I Want You'') , which starts Casas, Valverde and [[Clara Lago]], premiered in 2012.{{cite news|url=http://www.hola.com/cine/2012061959166/mario-casas-maria-valverde-tengo-ganas-de-ti/ |accessdate=2 November 2012 |language=Spanish |work=[[Hello (magazine)|Hola]] |title=Mario Casas, María Valverde y Clara Lago presentan 'Tengo ganas de ti' |date=19 June 2012}} In 2010 Casas starred ''[[Neon Meat|Carne de neon]]'' (''Neon Meat'') directed by [[Paco Cabezas]] later that year on 3 December 2010, ''[[Three Meters Above The Sky|Tres metros sobre el cielo]]'' (''Three Steps Above Heaven'') opposite [[María Valverde]]. It was eventually the highest-grossing Spanish film of the year.

Since 2011, Casas has starred the Antena 3 series ''[[El Barco (TV series)|El barco]]'' (''The Boat'') as Ulises Garmendia, opposite [[Blanca Suárez]]. He was ranked the second at the ''[[20 minutos]]'' list of sexiest Spanish actors.<ref name="20minutos">{{cite news|url=http://listas.20minutos.es/lista/los-actores-espanoles-mas-sexies-de-la-television-186153/ |title=Los Actores Españoles Mas Sexies De La Television |date=17 February 2011 |accessdate=2 November 2012 |language=Spanish}}</ref>

''[[Unit 7 (pelicula)|Grupo 7]]'' (''Unit 7'') by [[Alberto Rodríguez Librero|Alberto Rodriguez]]<ref>http://www.movingimage.us/visit/calendar/2014/04/04/detail/unit-7-grupo-7</ref> and the most anticipated sequel of the year ''[[Tengo ganas de ti]]'' (''I Want You''), which starts Casas, Valverde and [[Clara Lago]], premiered in 2012.{{cite news|url=http://www.hola.com/cine/2012061959166/mario-casas-maria-valverde-tengo-ganas-de-ti/ |accessdate=2 November 2012 |language=Spanish |work=[[Hello (magazine)|Hola]] |title=Mario Casas, María Valverde y Clara Lago presentan 'Tengo ganas de ti' |date=19 June 2012}}

Casas is set to star three [[2013 in film|films in 2013]] – First, he made his first film in English titled ''[[Eden (película)|Eden]]'' (''Eden'') with American actors [[Jessica Lowndes]], [[James Remar]], [[Sung Kang]], [[Diego Boneta]] directed by [[Shyam Madiraju]] in Malaysia in which I play felix the goalie. Later that year ''[[Ismael (pelicula)|Ismael]]'' (''Ismael'') by [[Marcelo Piñeyro]] and [[Álex de la Iglesia]]'s ''[[The Witches of Zugarramurdi|Las brujas de Zugarramurdi]]'' (''The Witches of Zugarramurdi'') opposite Hugo Silva,{{cite news|accessdate=2 November 2012 |language=Spanish |work=[[Hello (magazine)|Hola]] |date=28 September 2012 |url=http://www.hola.com/cine/2012092860947/hugo-silva-mario-casas-rodaje/ |title=Mario Casas y Hugo Silva, dos atractivos 'hechizados' en la nueva cinta de Álex de la Iglesia}}.

Currently during the late 2013 and early 2014 has been shooting between [[Colombia]] and [[Chile]], his second film project in English called ''[[The 33|Los 33]]'' (''The 33'') with [[Antonio Banderas]] and [[Juliette Binoche]], a story based on the [[2010 Copiapó mining accident|Chilean mining accident]] that occurred in 2010.<ref>http://us.hola.com/cine/2014020369502/antoniobanderas-mariocasas-mineros/</ref> It has also been confirmed the signing of Mario for the movie ''[[Palm Snow|Palmeras en la Nieve]]'' (''Palm Snow''), which will work with the director of the films ''[[Three Meters Above The Sky|Tres metros sobre el cielo]]'' (''Three Steps Above Heaven'') and ''[[Tengo ganas de ti]]'' (''I Want You''), and [[Fernando Gonzalez Molina]], In which he will play the main protagonist Killian.<ref>http://www.abc.es/cultura/cine/20130920/abci-mario-casas-protagoniza-palmeras-201309201101.html</ref>

== Personal life ==
Casas has four younger siblings – Sheila (born on 21 November 1987), Christian (born 1992), [[:es:Óscar Casas|Óscar]] (born on 21 September 1998) and Daniel (born on 27 April 2014).<ref name="antena">{{cite news|url=http://www.antena3.com/celebrities/famosos-espanoles/marios-casas-toma-dias-descanso-familia_2011072800031.html |title=Mario Casas se toma unos días de descanso en familia |date=28 July 2011 |accessdate=2 November 2012 |language=Spanish |work=[[Antena 3 (Spain)|Antena 3]]}}</ref> Christian and Óscar are also actors, while Sheilla is a law student.<ref name="elmundo">{{cite news|url=http://www.elmundo.es/elmundo/2011/03/01/television/1299010606.html |title=La hermana de Mario Casas asalta la banca de 'Allá tú' |date=3 March 2011 |accessdate=2 November 2012 |language=Spanish |work=[[El Mundo (Spain)|El mundo]]}}</ref> Born in [[A Coruña]], Galicia, Casas and his family moved to [[Barcelona]] in 1994.<ref name="pec"/> Having initially wanted to become a policeman, firefighter or a [[association football|football]] player,<ref name="bio">{{cite news|url=http://prensacorazon.com/mario-casas-biografia/ |work=www.prensacorazon.com |language=Spanish |title=Mario Casas Biografía |accessdate=2 November 2012}}</ref> he eventually decided to become an actor after he had enrolled the [[Cristina Rota|Cristina Rota School of Dramatic Arts]] and relocated to [[Madrid]], aged seventeen.<ref name="pec">{{cite news|url=http://www.puntoencuentrocomplutense.es/2009/06/entrevista-a-mario-casas/ |title=Entrevista a Mario Casas, en exclusiva |work=Punto de Encuentro |language=Spanish |accessdate=2 November 2012 |date=18 June 2009 |author=Redondo, David; Blázquez, Clara}}</ref> He is a supporter of [[Deportivo de La Coruña]] and [[FC Barcelona]].<ref name="pec"/>

He's currently dating Berta Vazquez.<ref>http://www.diariosur.es/gente-estilo/201409/19/berta-vazquez-nuevo-romance-20140919120636.html</ref> He also dated Spanish actress [[Maria Valverde]] for five years, until they split up in Spring 2014.<ref>http://los40.com/los40/2014/11/27/actualidad/1417085912_474486.html</ref>
