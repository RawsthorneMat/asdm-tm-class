'''Maria Maggenti''' (born c. 1962) is a film director and screenwriter for film and television.

She has been the [[script editor]] and has written many episodes of the American television series, ''[[Without a Trace]]'' (2003), but is perhaps best known for her feature film, ''[[The Incredibly True Adventure of Two Girls in Love]]'' (1995). Her film ''[[Puccini for Beginners]]'' was in competition at the [[Sundance Film Festival]] in January 2006. She was also an activist with [[AIDS Coalition to Unleash Power|ACT UP]] for many years. She attended [[Smith College]] and majored in Philosophy and [[Classics]].
